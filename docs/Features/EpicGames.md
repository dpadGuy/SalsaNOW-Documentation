**USING [--updateEpic] WILL REMOVE EPIC GAMES METADATA, MEANING GAMES WILL NOT APPEAR IN YOUR LIBRARY AS INSTALLED AFTER UPDATE.**<br>
**MAKE SURE YOU MAKE BACKUP OF YOUR METADATA, GAMES REMAIN INSTALLED ON YOUR SESSION IF YOU HAVE PERSISTENT STORAGE.**
